Unit Assessment System for Temple University

For the office of Institutional Research and Assessment and contributors who need to assess administrative and support units. The Unit Assessment System (UAS) tracks and mangages assessments for various units at Temple University annually. Unlike the SLAM system that was developed for academic assessments, UAS will be tailored to manage unit outcomes. This product will streamline the manual proccess in place for completing unit assessments.

I have become familiar with how a data center operates and is maintained through my experience at Temple University.

Aid-DD Application

For people who struggle with ADHD/ADD, the Aid-DD application is a time-management service that helps people organize their daily tasks, manage their time better and check-in with themselves throughout the day by using the app. Unlike other apps on the market that flood your phone with distracting notifications, our application uses a “check-in” time feature to minimize the number of notifications so you can stay on track without getting distracted.
